BigData News Analysis

The BigData News Analysis project is a scalable data analytics solution designed to extract and interpret trends in news content using modern technologies such as Python, PySpark, and interactive visualizations.
It automates the collection of article headlines from multiple news websites, validates the accessibility of each source, and processes the text in a distributed environment. After performing text cleaning and normalization, the system efficiently counts the most frequent words and presents the results through a word cloud and bar chart. Additionally, the project exports the results in JSON format, enabling seamless integration with external dashboards and providing a clear, up-to-date overview of the most discussed topics in the media.
